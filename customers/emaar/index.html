Emaar Square Mall, a bustling shopping destination in İstanbul, Turkey, faced the challenge of attracting and retaining visitors in an increasingly competitive retail landscape. They recognized the crucial role of technology in enhancing the customer experience and driving loyalty. By implementing a robust and user-friendly BuWiFi solution, Emaar Square Mall achieved significant success in boosting dwell time, gathering valuable customer insights, and building stronger relationships with their patrons.

Challenges:

Emaar Square Mall partnered with Junivo to deploy a high-performance WiFi network throughout the entire mall premises. The solution included

  • Limited Customer Engagement: Prior to the Wi-Fi implementation, [Mall Name] lacked a direct channel to communicate with visitors and provide personalized offers.
  • Slow and Unreliable Connectivity: Existing Wi-Fi infrastructure was inadequate, leading to frustrating slow speeds and frequent disconnections, impacting the customer experience.
  • Lack of Data-Driven Insights: The mall lacked a comprehensive understanding of visitor behavior, making it difficult to tailor marketing campaigns and optimize operational efficiency.
Solution:

Teknosa partnered with Junivo to deploy a high-performance WiFi network throughout the entire mall premises. The solution included

  • High-Speed, Reliable Connectivity: A robust and scalable Wi-Fi network with high bandwidth and strong signal strength, ensuring seamless connectivity for all visitors.
  • Captive Portal with Guest Authentication: A user-friendly captive portal that required a simple email address or social media login for Wi-Fi access.
  • Advanced Analytics Dashboard: A comprehensive analytics platform providing real-time and historical data on visitor demographics, dwell times, popular areas within the mall, and Wi-Fi usage patterns.
  • Targeted Marketing Capabilities: The ability to send personalized messages and promotions to visitors based on their interests, browsing history, and in-mall location.
